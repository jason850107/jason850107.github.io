 What’s MoFi? 

MoFi is worldwide Mono Wi-Fi Network system. 

 What does MoFi try to do?

MoFi is trying to co-centralize global Wi-Fi network into P2P utility network system, shared and accessed by anyone in the world.

 What are the principles of MoFi system? 

The principles of MoFi system is of the members, by the members and for the members. 

 What’s MoFi coin?
